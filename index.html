Hey, I'm Emily

Emily is an Ottawa-based designer who creates confident "it" brands with her flair for elegance and innovation.

Throughout her childhood, high school, and until now, she has always used creativity to overcome obstacles and create uplifting designs with a story in mind.

To get more of an insight on Emily, here are a few fun facts: She craves and acknowledges a product for their packaging and cannot handle but to buy it. In addition to her shopping habits, she enjoys exercising, travelling, watching shows, listening to music, dining, and endulging in a good magazine.
